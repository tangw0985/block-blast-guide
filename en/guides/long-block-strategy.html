Best Way to Place Long Blocks in Block Blast

A long bar is restrictive because every cell must fit on one straight line. Jagged boards quickly remove all valid placements.

Updated June 23, 2026Intermediate
Quick answer: Keep at least one five-cell horizontal or vertical lane and use the bar to finish a line instead of filling a random open area.

Reserve a lane early

Avoid placing isolated cells across every row. Compact stacking keeps entire rows or columns available.

Horizontal or vertical?

Choose the direction that advances a clear. If neither scores, protect the center and your 3x3 zone.

No five-cell lane remains

Use the other pieces first to clear a row or column and recreate a straight path.
