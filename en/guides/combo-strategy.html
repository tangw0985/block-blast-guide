Block Blast Combo Strategy: Double and Multi-Line Clears

A strong multi-line clear comes from one placement completing a row and a column at the same time.

Updated June 23, 2026Intermediate
Quick answer: Identify nearly completed rows and columns, then protect their crossing point for a small piece that can finish both.

Find the crossing point

Look for rows and columns missing only one or two cells. Their shared area is the most valuable target.

Why small pieces matter

A 1x1, 1x2, or small L piece can complete exact gaps that large pieces cannot reach.

When not to wait

If your board is losing large-piece space, take the safe single clear. Survival is more valuable than a risky future combo.

Practice routine

  • Mark two potential lines.
  • Find their crossing area.
  • Save a precise finishing piece.
  • Compare immediate space with delayed combo value.
