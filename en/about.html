About Block Blast Guide

Learn about the editorial goals and independence of Block Blast Guide.

Our goal

We explain specific Block Blast situations with original diagrams, direct answers, and practical steps.

Editorial principles

  • Answer a real gameplay question on each page.
  • Separate tested strategy from opinion.
  • Correct broken links and inaccurate guidance.
  • Avoid promising guaranteed scores.

Independent website

This is an independent fan-made strategy website and is not affiliated with the game developer or publisher.
